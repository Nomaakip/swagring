about swagring

about

swagring is a webring for cool sites!

it was created in 2025 and is managed by nomaakip.

please submit a prthe repo for issues/suggestions.

joining

requirements

your website must:
  • be free of nsfw, racist, sexist, homophobic/transphobic and ableist content
  • be hand-coded (no google sites, straw.page, carrd or wix)
  • have some sort of content (even a simple "under construction page" is fine!)

if your website fits those requirements, submit a pull request here! simply add a new json entry at the bottom of sites.json:

{ "name": "<name>", "about": "<about>" "url": "<url>" }

you can also dm me on discord (@hacks.guide) and ask to get added to the webring!
